# Stefanos Voikos

Stefanos Voikos is a research assistant in the Network Management and Optimal Design Laboratory at the National Technical University of Athens. He received his Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens, Greece, in 2023. He is currently pursuing his MSc degree in Data Science and Machine Learning at the National Technical University of Athens. His research focuses on radio and compute resource allocation in wireless federated learning networks.
