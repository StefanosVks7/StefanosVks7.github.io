About Me

Stefanos Voikos is a Research Assistant at the Network Management and Optimal Design Laboratory at the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA). He received his Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from NTUA in 2023 and is currently pursuing an MSc in Data Science and Machine Learning at the same institution. His research interests focus on radio and compute resource allocation in wireless federated learning systems, with emphasis on optimization, distributed machine learning, and efficient resource management in next-generation wireless networks. His work also involves applying machine learning and optimization techniques to improve system performance, scalability, and energy efficiency in communication networks.
